Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join Etcetera.in? +

Simply visit our club during orientation week or contact us through our social media channels. We welcome all MGMCET students regardless of their coding experience level.

Do I need prior coding experience? +

Not at all! We have programs for beginners as well as advanced coders. Our mentorship program pairs newcomers with experienced members to help you get started.

What programming languages do you focus on? +

We cover a wide range including Python, JavaScript, Java, C++, and emerging technologies like blockchain, AI/ML, and web development frameworks.

Are there any membership fees? +

Etcetera.in is completely free for all MGMCET students. We believe in making quality tech education accessible to everyone in our community.

How often do you organize events? +

We organize weekly coding sessions, monthly workshops, and quarterly major events like hackathons and tech fests. Stay tuned to our social media for updates!
